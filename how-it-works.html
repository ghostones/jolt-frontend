How JOLT Works

JOLT is designed to be simple, predictable, and easy to use. No downloads. No accounts. No unnecessary complexity.

Here's exactly what to expect.

1. Open JOLT in Your Browser

JOLT works directly in your browser. You don't need to install an app, create an account, or share personal details.

2. Read the Community Rules

Before starting, users are expected to understand the basic rules. These guidelines exist to keep conversations respectful and comfortable.

3. Start a Conversation

When you begin, JOLT connects you with another user for a one-on-one video conversation.

  • Each interaction is random
  • Each conversation is temporary
  • You are always free to move on

4. Skip or Report When Needed

Not every match will be a good one. At any time, you can skip to the next conversation or report behavior that breaks the rules.

5. Why JOLT Feels Different

Many random chat platforms prioritize speed and volume. JOLT prioritizes comfort, clarity, and safety.

The experience is designed to feel calmer — so users don't feel the need to constantly skip or stay on edge.

JOLT isn't about endless randomness. It's about making random conversation usable again.

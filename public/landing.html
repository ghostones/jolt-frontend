JOLT – The Future of Random Chat

JOLT is a next-generation random chat platform that helps people connect instantly with strangers in a safe, private, and abuse-resistant environment.

Built with modern security practices and AI moderation, JOLT removes bots, prevents scraping, and actively protects users from harmful content.

How JOLT Works

Users are matched randomly for real-time text or video conversations using WebRTC technology. Sessions are protected using secure tokens and anti-abuse rate limits to ensure fair usage.

Safety & Privacy First

JOLT does not publicly index conversations or profiles. Moderation actions are logged securely, and repeat offenders are automatically removed from the platform.

Our goal is to create a respectful and enjoyable space where users can talk freely without fear of harassment or exploitation.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

JOLT (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect data when you use JOLT.

1. Information We Collect

  • Username and account details you provide during signup
  • Basic profile information (age, interests, gender – optional)
  • Session and technical data for security and abuse prevention
  • Payment confirmation data from payment providers (we do not store card details)

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide random chat and matching services
  • To enforce community rules and prevent abuse
  • To improve security, performance, and reliability
  • To process premium subscriptions

3. Chat Content & Moderation

Chats may be analyzed automatically for safety and moderation purposes. We do not sell chat content. Moderation data is securely stored and access is restricted.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

JOLT uses cookies or local storage only for login sessions and basic functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for profiling.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Payment providers (Razorpay, PayPal)
  • Analytics and security services
  • Advertising partners (Google AdSense)

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect user data. However, no online system can be 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

JOLT is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Your Rights

You may request account deletion or data access by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of JOLT means you accept the updated policy.

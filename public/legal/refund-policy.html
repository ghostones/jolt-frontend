Refund & Cancellation Policy

JOLT offers digital subscription services. Once a premium plan is activated, it cannot be cancelled or refunded, except in cases of technical failure or accidental duplicate payments.

Refund Eligibility

  • Duplicate payment for the same plan
  • Payment deducted but premium not activated

Non-Refundable

  • Used subscription time
  • User dissatisfaction after usage

How to Request a Refund

Email us at support@joltchat.org with your payment ID within 48 hours.

Approved refunds will be processed within 7 business days.

JOLT Community Rules

JOLT is a safe, non‑adult social chat app. To keep everyone safe:

  • No nudity or sexual content.
  • No harassment, hate speech, or bullying.
  • No sharing of private contact info (phone, WhatsApp, Instagram, etc.).
  • No illegal content or activities.
  • Respect others; report and block bad behavior.

Violations may lead to kicks, temporary bans, or permanent bans.
